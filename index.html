About NxNW

NxNW Surf, founded by Frank Crippen, aims to mindfully introduce people to the ocean through surfing. Dedicated to bringing you the highest quality surf gear with the lowest carbon footprint, NxNW showcases the underground talents of the Pacific Northwest surf community. Owned by Tait Trautman, who was born and raised in Washington State and learned to surf in Washington in 1977, NxNW Surf Co aims to supply the hardcore surfer, skater, and snowboarder with the best quality, reasonably priced products available. By partnering with sustainably manufactured brands and craftsmen, NxNW provides a meaningful retail experience that supports the local surf community and the planet. Located in Port Angeles since its opening in 1995, NxNW Surf serves as both your first and last stop for every surf trip on the Olympic Peninsula. The North Coast of the Olympic Peninsula, with its last remaining stretch of true wilderness in the continental United States, deserves utmost respect. NxNW believes in maintaining the delicate balance between supplying surfers with the gear they need while preserving this wilderness. Many spots on the North Coast are on private property and can be closed to the general public if rules are not obeyed and trash is left behind. NxNW encourages visitors to do their part by obeying all rules and packing out more trash than they bring in. While they don't give out maps, NxNW believes the search for these spots will bring the reward. Thanks for shopping at NxNW, Tait Trautman.
